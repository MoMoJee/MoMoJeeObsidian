# ABSTRACT

Finger joint angle measurements are important for the fields such as treatment of joint diseases, health monitoring, wearable devices, etc. As an effective detecting device, the joint angle monitoring sensor should have wide measurement angle range, and high sensitivity. In addition, the sensor should have good skin affinity, so as to enhance the degree of comfort for the user. In this paper, high-sensitivity bending angle sensing based on tapered plastic optical fiber (POF) configuration is proposed. The sensing performance is enhanced by tapering the POF and bending the POF taper to form a double U-shaped configuration then encapsulated with Polydimethylsiloxane (PDMS) film, therefore, the POF taper will be bent at three positions simultaneously, so that the bend induced loss will increase. The experimental preparation process of tapered POF and PDMS film package are presented in detail. The influence of taper waist diameter, and bending diameter on the bending loss has been investigated and optimized to enhance the angle sensitivity of the double U-shaped configuration. The results show that the sensitivity can reach $0.070\mathrm{dB} / ^{\circ}$ with a linearity of 0.995 in a wide bending angle range of $0 - 105^{\circ}$ . The POF sensor is then applied for the joint bending detection. The experimental results show that the sensor can quickly respond to small pressures, and the variation of bending angles can be distinguished in the detection of joint bending. The proposed POF bending sensor shows the advantages of wide measurement angle range, high sensitivity, low cost and good portability, and shows important potential applications in the fields such as human joint movement detection.

手指关节角度测量对于关节疾病治疗、健康监测、可穿戴设备等领域具有重要意义。作为一种有效的检测装置，关节角度监测传感器应具备较宽的测量角度范围和高灵敏度。此外，传感器还需具有良好的皮肤亲和性，以提升使用者的舒适度。本文提出了一种基于锥形塑料光纤（POF）结构的高灵敏度弯曲角度传感方案。通过将POF拉锥，并将锥区弯曲成双U形结构后封装于聚二甲基硅氧烷（PDMS）薄膜中，使POF锥区在三个位置同时发生弯曲，从而增加弯曲引起的损耗。文中详细介绍了锥形POF及PDMS薄膜封装的实验制备过程，并研究了锥腰直径和弯曲直径对弯曲损耗的影响，通过优化这些参数提升了双U形结构的角度灵敏度。实验结果表明，在 $0 - 105^{\circ}$ 的宽弯曲角度范围内，灵敏度可达 $0.070\mathrm{dB} / \mathrm{°}$ ，线性度为0.995。随后将该POF传感器应用于关节弯曲检测，实验显示传感器能快速响应微小压力，并能在关节弯曲检测中区分不同弯曲角度的变化。所提出的POF弯曲传感器具有测量角度范围宽、灵敏度高、成本低、便携性好等优势，在人体关节运动检测等领域展现出重要的应用潜力。

# 2. Design of sensing unit

The double U-shaped tapered POF bending sensor proposed in this paper is realized by bending a section of tapered optical fiber into two U-shaped structures with opposite bending directions $(180^{\circ})$ , as shown in Fig. 1. The sensing principle is based on light intensity modulation, so the purpose of pulling the POF is to increase the evanescent field effect of the transmitted light in the optical fiber and improve its sensitivity. The bending positions would be set as the center around the taper waist, the bending of the PDMS will lead to the bending of the three POF taper sections simultaneously, therefore, the bending induced loss would increase. 

# 3. Fabrication of double U-shaped conical POF sensing element

It can be seen from Fig. 1 that the double U-shaped bending structure has two U-shaped structural elements. The preparation of the double U-shaped tapered POF bending sensing unit can be divided into three steps. Firstly, the tapered POF needs to be prepared using standard experimental preparation methods, then the double U-shaped bending heat-setting process is performed on the tapered POF. Finally, the sensing unit is packaged with PDMS. 

The commonly used methods for experimental preparation of conical POF include electrospinning method, chemical etching method, pulling method and melting cone pulling method [17,18]. Among them, the surface of the tapered plastic optical fiber obtained by the fusion taper method is relatively smooth, and the transition area is also smooth and uniform. The fusion taper machine can also control the taper structure more accurately by adjusting the taper temperature and taper speed, reducing unnecessary interference factors and experimental errors, so that the performance of the optical fiber bend sensor is more stable. In this paper, the conical POF was prepared by the melting cone method.

Repeatability refers to the degree of inconsistency of the characteristic curve obtained when the sensor element is measured multiple times in full range according to the same stroke (increase or decrease of bending angle) under the same working condition. If the characteristic curves of multiple measurements are more coincident, the repeatability of the sensor is better and the error is smaller. Repeatability can be expressed as the limit value of random error under a certain confidence probability, as a percentage of full scale output 

$$
\zeta_ {R} = \frac {(2 \sim 3) S _ {\max}}{y _ {\max} - y _ {\min}} \times 100 \% \tag{2}
$$

Among them, $\xi_{R}$ is repeatability, and $S_{\mathrm{max}}$ is the maximum value of the sample standard deviation in the whole measurement range. The standard deviation is calculated according to Bessel formula 

$$
S = \sqrt {\frac {\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \left(y _ {i} - \bar {y}\right) ^ {2}}{n - 1}} \tag {3}
$$

重复性是指在相同工作条件下，按相同行程（弯曲角度的增加或减少）对传感元件进行全量程多次测量时，所得特性曲线的不一致程度。若多次测量的特性曲线重合度越高，则传感器的重复性越好，误差越小。重复性可用特定置信概率下随机误差的极限值表示，以满量程输出的百分比计。

$$
\zeta_ {R} = \frac {(2 \sim 3) S _ {\max}}{y _ {\max} - y _ {\min}} \times 100 \% \tag{2}
$$

$$
S = \sqrt {\frac {\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \left(y _ {i} - \bar {y}\right) ^ {2}}{n - 1}} \tag {3}
$$

# 6. Conclusion

This article proposes a double U-shaped tapered POF structure encapsulating PDMS film for high-sensitivity bending angle sensing. The sensing performance is enhanced by arranging tapering the POF and forming a double U-shaped configuration in the tapered section then encapsulated with PDMS film, therefore, the POF taper will be bent at three positions so that the bend induced loss will increase. The results show that the sensitivity can reach $0.070\mathrm{dB} / \circ$ with a linearity of 0.995 in a wide bending angle range of $0 - 105^{\circ}$ . Based on this sensor, an adaptive detection of knuckle bending activities is carried out. The double U-shaped tapered POF bending sensor can quickly respond to small finger pressure changes when pressed, and can distinguish the bending angle and bending speed in the detection of knuckle bending. The experimental results show that the designed POF bending sensor based on the tapered structure has the advantages of wide measurement angle range, high sensitivity, low cost and good portability. The proposed sensor shows the feasibility of potential applications in the fields such as human joint movement detection.

# 6.结论

本文提出了一种封装PDMS薄膜的双U形锥形POF结构，用于高灵敏度弯曲角度传感。通过将POF设计为锥形并在锥形段形成双U形结构，然后封装PDMS薄膜，从而增强传感性能；因此，POF锥形部分将在三个位置发生弯曲，使得弯曲引起的损耗增加。结果表明，在 $0 - 105^{\circ}$ 的宽弯曲角度范围内，灵敏度可达 $0.070\mathrm{dB} / ^{\circ}$ ，线性度为0.995。基于该传感器，实现了对指关节弯曲活动的自适应检测。这种双U形锥形POF弯曲传感器在受压时能快速响应微小的手指压力变化，并能在检测指关节弯曲时区分弯曲角度和弯曲速度。实验结果表明，所设计的基于锥形结构的POF弯曲传感器具有测量角度范围宽、灵敏度高、成本低和便携性好等优点。该传感器展现了在人体关节运动检测等领域的潜在应用可行性。
